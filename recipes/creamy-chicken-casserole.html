Creamy Chicken Casserole

Description

Find the recipe below!

60 minutes preparation
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of The Vegetarian Butcher Chicken Pieces
  • 600 grams of potatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 250 grams of mushroom
  • oliver oil
  • 200 grams green olives with garlic (seeds removed)
  • 6 springs of fresh thyme
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 200 ml dry white wine
  • 300 ml vegetable stock
  • 300 grams cooking cream
  • salt and pepper
  • Extra: a large fireproof casserole with lid

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Wash and halve the potatoes, cut the large ones into quarters. Peel and chop the onions. Clean the mushrooms. Halve the small ones and quarter the larger ones.
  3. Heat a dash of olive oil in a large, heatproof frying pan. Add the vegan chicken pieces, season with salt and pepper and bake for 5 minutes until brown, do not turn! Add the onions and potatoes, mix and cook for a further 3 minutes. Turn every now and then.
  4. Add the olives and fresh thyme springs.
  5. Sprinkle the flour over the ingredients in the casserole, mix well and pour in the wine and stock. Bring to boil. Cover and place the casserole in the oven for 30-40 minutes.
  6. After 20 minutes, stir in the mushrooms and leave the casserole on the oven for another 10 minutes. Check if the potatoes are cooked. If not, let them cook a little longer.
  7. Then put the frying pan on the stove. Mix in the cooking cream and cook for a few minutes. Taste and season with salt and pepper.
