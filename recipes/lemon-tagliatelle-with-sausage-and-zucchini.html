Lemon Tagliatelle with Sausage & Zucchini

Description

Find the recipe below!

55 minutes preparation
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 250g chipolata sausages
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 courgettes
  • 200ml long-life whipped cream
  • 1 lemon
  • 125g Parmiggiano Reggiano cheese
  • 300g tagliatelle

Steps

  1. Cut open the skin of the sausages with a sharp knife and remove the meat. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Break the meat into pieces, add and fry until loose.
  2. Cook the tagliatelle according to the package directions.
  3. Meanwhile, finely chop the garlic. Cut the courgettes into cubes of about 2 cm. Add the garlic and zucchini to the meat and fry for 3 minutes while stirring. Add the cream and stir.
  4. Drain the pasta and toss through the sauce. Scrub the lemon, grate the yellow zest over the pasta and squeeze half of the fruit over it. Stir in half of the cheese and season with pepper.
  5. Divide the pasta among 4 plates and sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
